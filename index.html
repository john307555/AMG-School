About Us

Al Mawakeb Al Garhoud is a premier educational institution in Dubai, founded in 1979. Known for its academic excellence and holistic approach, the school serves a diverse student body, offering programs that foster intellectual, social, and emotional development.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide a nurturing and challenging environment where students are empowered to achieve their highest potential through critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.

Vision: To shape responsible global citizens who are equipped with the skills, knowledge, and values to thrive in an interconnected world.

Academics

Our comprehensive curriculum includes:

  • Early Childhood Education: Focusing on foundational skills and social development.
  • Primary Education: A balanced approach to literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts.
  • Secondary Education: Advanced courses in sciences, humanities, and languages, preparing students for higher education.
  • International Programs: Partnerships with global institutions and participation in IB and AP programs.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities that enhance student development:

  • Sports: Football, basketball, swimming, and more.
  • Arts: Music, drama, painting, and photography clubs.
  • STEM Clubs: Robotics, coding, and science fairs.
  • Community Service: Volunteering programs and leadership initiatives.

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art campus includes:

  • Modern classrooms equipped with smart technology.
  • Fully equipped science and computer labs.
  • Extensive library with digital resources.
  • Sports complex, swimming pool, and gymnasium.
  • Dedicated arts and music studios.
