About Us

Al Mawakeb Al Garhoud, founded in 1979, has established itself as one of Dubai's premier educational institutions. With a commitment to academic excellence and character development, the school serves a diverse student body, representing over 80 nationalities.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: We aim to provide a nurturing environment where students can excel academically and grow as individuals.

Vision: Our vision is to produce responsible global citizens equipped with skills, knowledge, and values.

Academics

We offer a dynamic curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students at every level:

  • Early Childhood Education: Play-based learning.
  • Primary Education: Core subjects and literacy.
  • Secondary Education: Specialized courses for higher education.
  • International Programs: IB and AP pathways.

Extracurricular Activities

Our extracurricular programs nurture talents and build leadership skills:

  • Sports: Football, basketball, swimming.
  • Performing Arts: Music, drama, dance.
  • STEM Clubs: Robotics, coding competitions.

Our Facilities

Our campus includes:

  • Smart classrooms with advanced technology.
  • Modern science and computer labs.
  • Extensive library and sports complex.
